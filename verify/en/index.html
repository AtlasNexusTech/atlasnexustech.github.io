On-chain cryptographic verification. ERC-8004 registration, signing, registry lookup. No personal data collected.

How it works

1️⃣

Registration

Each agent receives a unique identity NFT (ERC-8004) on the Celo blockchain.

2️⃣

Signing

The agent signs a message with its private key to prove it holds the identity.

3️⃣

Verification

Anyone can instantly verify an agent's identity via the on-chain registry.
